# About MoSAIC-P38

MoSAIC-P38 is the result of collaborative research at the **Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (LBNL)**.

## The Project
The project aims to advance the state of computer architecture by creating a tightly coupled system that leverages the strengths of both traditional von Neumann computing (RISC-V) and neuromorphic computing (SNN). This hybrid approach is critical for the next generation of AI-driven scientific discovery.

## The Team
Developed by the **Computer Architecture Group (CAG)** within the **Applied Mathematics & Computational Research (AMCR)** division at LBNL.

## Open Source
MoSAIC-P38 is open source. You can find the full codebase, including RTL and simulation tools, on GitHub:
[https://github.com/lbnlcomputerarch/MoSAIC-P38](https://github.com/lbnlcomputerarch/MoSAIC-P38)
